Service Schedule

9:00 AM Traditional Service

This service is traditional at its core with traditional hymns, Gospel-centered preaching. This service includes a focus on children and families in the first quarter of an hour, allowing them to participate in the important key portions of our worship, all prior to being dismissed for Sunday School or Club 4-5. Excellent music is provided throughout the year by our adult, youth and children choirs, our adult handbell and children handbell choirs and special guests.

This service is also LIVESTREAMED ONLINE (via our Website and YouTube Channel) and is a great option for those who prefer to watch from home. Links to these services can be found HERE on our website or on our YouTube Channel. If you happen to miss it at 9:00 am, no problem! Videos of these services can be viewed at a later time or date too!

10:30 AM Alternative Service

This service offers a contemporary worship experience centering around music and sharing God's good news. Service music is led by Minneapolis based musician, Dan Schwartz, and includes a variety of other talented local musicians, and provides a casual worship experience filled with a spirit of freedom, flexibility and joy. If worship has not been a regular part of your life, this may be a new connecting point and a great opportunity to make worship part of your weekly routine. This service also includes a greater focus on children and families in the first quarter of the hour, allowing them to participate in the important key portions of our worship, all prior to being dismissed for Sunday School or Club 4-5. Our youth, children's and handbell choirs along with special guests add to the music throughout the year.

Prayer List/Requests

All prayer requests are shared with pastors and ministry staff and are kept confidential.

You may indicate if you want the prayer to be included in the Prayer Chain (A chain of members who also pray for your loved one, prayers are kept confidential) or on the Prayer List which is published to the congregation.

Worship Assistants

Volunteers are used in a variety of ways on Sunday mornings. If you are available to help with any of these ministries, please contact the church office.

During Worship:

Ushers

Assist with distribution of bulletins, collection of offerings, invite for communion, attendance counting, and general clean-up following service.

Scripture Readers

Read aloud the assigned scripture to the congregation.

Communion Servers

Help distribute communion to the congregation.

Communion Preparers

Set-up communion elements, place on credence table in sanctuary, refill or clean-up communion elements, fill/empty baptism font as needed, and place baptism supplies on altar as needed.

Audio Visual Assistant

Monitor sound board, advance prepared Power Point presentations, implement lighting changes as needed, and run camera/projector as needed.

Outside Worship

Greeters

Welcome and greet all attendees as they arrive. Help answer attendees questions as needed.

Drivers

Drive the church van/bus to pick up 9:30 am worship attendees in local area. Routes are pre-determined based on need.

Coffee Servers

Set up trays of treats, make and serve coffee during fellowship times between services.

Welcome Desk Assistant

Sit at the Welcome Desk between services to assist those with questions.
