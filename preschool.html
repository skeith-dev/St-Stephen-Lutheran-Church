Curriculum

We provide a Christian based educational experience for children of all faiths and backgrounds beginning at age three. We offer a curriculum supporting the basic building blocks of education while providing opportunities for your child to take giant steps forward as they grow.

Our teachers and staff are committed to giving your child a wonderful adventure with their first experience in education. We invite your to explore our website and learn more about our preschool.

2023-2024 Registration

Registration is on a first-come, first-serve basis.

Submitting a registration form DOES NOT guarantee your class placement. You will be notified within 2 days of your child's placement. Once notified, you will need to pay a $75 non-refundable registration fee.

Registration Fee: The non-refundable registration fee is $75 and needs to be paid within 2 days of being notified of your child's class placement. You may pay online at: www.ststephen.net/online-giving-payments, mail in a check, or bring a check (or cash) to St. Stephen Lutheran Church & Preschool, 8400 France Ave S, Bloomington, MN 55431-1519.
