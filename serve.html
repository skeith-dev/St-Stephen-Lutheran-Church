Pledging

Faithful God, Faith-Filled Community is our theme of our fall stewardship appeal, preparing for 2023.

As St. Stephen looks ahead to 2023, though the pandemic has subsided, we know that political differences still divide. Injustice continues to impact people' lives. Many lack food and others continue to live without homes. Following Christ's lead into the world with faith, St. Stephen will continue to follow the same bold course moved forward in 2022.

With an additional $70,000 in giving in 2023 we will build on a strong foundation of vibrant ministry.

We will boldly step forward through our strategic plan:

  1. Strengthening community within St. Stephen.
  2. Deeping our connections outside of St. Stephen.
  3. Deepening our understanding of our community outside of St. Stephen (and our place in it)!
  4. SUSTAIN MINISTRY EXCELLENCE as we expand in these areas, we build on a strong foundation of vibrant ministry

We will accomplish this by investing in and retaining our excellent staff and our significant ministries. We are excited about all of the ministry opportunities we have committed to and to which God is calling us! We are also excited to grow in our generosity to God and to one another!
