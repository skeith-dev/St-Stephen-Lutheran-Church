About Us

St. Stephen Lutheran Church is a member of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America (ELCA).

We are located at the corner of 84th Street and France Avenue in Bloomington, Minnesota.

Established in 1959, St. Stephen offers individuals and families of all ages many opportunities to grow in their faith, tell of God's love, and serve others in the congregation, the community, and the world.
